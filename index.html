About Me

Name:Maryam Aslam

Profession: Aspiring Instructional Designer

My name is Maryam Aslam, and I currently reside in Gainesville, Florida, where I am pursuing my Ph.D. in Educational Technology at the University of Florida. I am passionate about exploring how technology can transform teaching and learning experiences, particularly through the use of game-based assessments, immersive learning environments, and data-driven feedback systems.

Before beginning my doctoral studies, I worked as a Lecturer in Computer Science at Bahria University, where I taught courses across computing and technology disciplines. My teaching experiences deeply shaped my interest in developing innovative ways to make complex technical subjects more engaging and accessible for students.

Through my current research, I aim to design and evaluate educational technologies that foster active learning, creativity, and self-regulated learning among students. My goal is to bridge the gap between technical innovation and pedagogical practice, ensuring that educational tools genuinely enhance student understanding and motivation.
